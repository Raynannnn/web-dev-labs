Our Story

RayJos Café began with two college sweethearts who shared the same passion: coffee and connection. Between late-night study sessions and early morning classes, they found joy in brewing cups together and sharing them with friends. What started as a simple hobby during their campus days soon grew into a dream—to create a space where coffee could bring people closer. Every cup at RayJos Café supports local farmers, every sip carries their story, and every visit is a reminder that love and community are best enjoyed over coffee.
